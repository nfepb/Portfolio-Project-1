Co-op

Co-op or Co-operative games push the participants to play together in order to achieve or reach a common goal. The opponent in this type of game is usually the game itself (games like Pandemic or Forbidden Island).

Area Control

Area Control games usually use mechanics of incentivizing players to control a large territory and players try to take control over territories controlled by other players (games like Risk or Small World).

Deck Builders

Deck Builders are games that make primarily use of cards. Usually, players start with a limited number of cards, but as the game progresses, players are able to acquire more or better cards, allowing more options (games like Star Realms of Dominion).

Combat

Combat games are among the eldest type of game still played to this day. Players usually have to defeat one another or defeat another party to win. Strategy can often be a predominant component in this type of game (games like chess or Blood Rage).

Deck Builders

In a Deck Building game, the main element of gameplay is to construct the best possible deck of cards. The deck is being built by the several players during the game. It usually involves some form of currency that allow the players to acquire new or better cards than the ones they originally start with at the start of the game. This type of game is most often accessible to any type of player. With all the players starting with similar cards, or cards of similar strength, there is most of the time an element of randomness involved, allowing a fair chance of winning to any player, regardless of the skill.
